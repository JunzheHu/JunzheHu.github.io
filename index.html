    COMPETITION¶

    NEXTORS Innovation Team - Chongqing University, Chongqing, China

    Team Leader, 01/2021-07/2021

    Project 1: Design and Implementation of Multifunctional Arrow Throwing Robot (ROBOCON 2021)

    • Adopted a crank slider structure to take arrows and implemented the recognition of two colors cylinders and competition arrow and ranging based on Yolov3 and binocular camera.

    • Completed the communication between the self-designed 3 DoF grasping robot board and PC, and picked the center of gravity of the arrow automatically. Realized accurate throwing according to the distance of visual ranging feedback.

    • Led the team to participate in ROBOCON2021 competition and won the second prize nationwide.

    Team Leader, 07/2021-09/2021

    Project 2: Tetris Intelligent Grasping Robot (4th China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition)

    • Utilized xARM 6 six-axis manipulator to implement the fetching and placing path planning of 7 types of Tetris and device communication under Linux system. Extracted the contours of Tetris with different colors through HSV image processing, and recognized different types of Tetris and the position coordinates to be clamped. Assembled the scattered Tetris into regular geometric shapes.

    • Implemented a seamless reconstruction of 27 random pieces of Tetris using a pneumatic clamp in 52 seconds, and won the second prize in China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition.
